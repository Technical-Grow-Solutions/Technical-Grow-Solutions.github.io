TGS-Tiny®

Welcome, to the smallest IoT-device, within Technical Grow Solutions line of smart devices, called: TGS-Tiny®

TGS-Tiny® is a automation combination of the most basic actions for your growth.

One secured multilingual web-based control and meassurement, for one room, of:

  • Temperature; with control of a heater and/or cooler.
  • Humidity; with control of a humidifier and/or dehumidifier.
  • Light; timer controlled.

Further it helps you to compile your waterscheme by a combination of your own compiled cycle-nutrient chart with the device's measurements: pH, EC and water temperature.

Notify you about critical or informative situations on WhatsApp.

In short, an all basic device for those who have their own grow tent or cabinet.

    Include:
    • Support of automatic updates with the latest firmware from our TGS® company server, up to and including 3 years after the online purchase date.
    • Free and anonymously access to online:
      • Evolving document, with the comprehensive plant training consisting of simple text with pics, for working on the spot like a pro.
      • Is your question not answered? ...there's 'your' community forum to post your questions.
      • Pool of files, produced and shared, again anonymously, by the community, consisting of cycle-nutrient charts for a variety of different plant species.
